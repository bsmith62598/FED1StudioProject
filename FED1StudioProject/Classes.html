So You Want To Be an Adventurer

Here at Dragon's Focus we have 13 different classes for you to take. Each of which offers 3 different variations. The first being for beginners, the second for a little more experienced individuals, and the third being for those who are very experienced. Below is a list of classes we offer and a brief description of them.

Artificer

The Artificer is a master of invention, they use thier ingenuity and a little bit of magic to turn seemingly mundane items into extraordinary ones.

Barbarian

The Barbarian is a savage warrior who can channel thier primitive background to go into a rage. Rivaling the most fierce of all beasts and men.

Bard

The Bard may not be exatcly what you think when adventure is brought up, however they are very important. The Bard has a wide array of inspiring magic which echos the music of creation.

Cleric

The Cleric is a warrior that is usually a champion of thier god or faith. They tap into the power thier god has to bring doom, or to save entire cities at once.

Druid

A Druid is a warrior of the planet. They are quite in-tune with the power of nature itself. They use that power to take on the form of different animals.

Fighter

The fighter doesn't have much in the ways of magic to back them up. However, they are masters of martial combat. They are skilled with nearly every weapon.

Monk

A Monk is a master of martial combat but without weapons. They harness the power of thier own body and spirit in the pursuit of perfection of body and spirit.

Paladin

Paladins are very similar to Clerics in the sense that they are provided power from thier god in desperate times. Paladins however are bound to an oath they take upon becoming a paladin.

Ranger

Rangers are masters of ranged weapon fighting. They are very versatile in almost any combat situation. No matter the terrain or enemy, a Ranger knows how to handle it.

Rogue

Rogues are masters of stealth, they stalk thier prey until the perfect moment presents itself for them to strike. They often use lots of trikery and deadly precision when striking.

Sorcerer

A Sorcerer is a spellcaster who draws on inherent magic from a gift of bloodline.

Warlock

A Warlock is a wielder of magic that is derived from a bargain with an extraplaner entity.

Wizard

Wizards are scholarly magic users capable of manipulating the structures of reality. Unlike other spellcasters Wizards can learn just about any spell.
